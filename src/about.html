Who we are:

ix.kw is a (not-for-profit) neutral Internet Exchange Point based in Kuwait enabling Provide high speed, reliable, resilient IP traffic exchange facilities for both local and international organization.

Our Vision:

The vision of IX.KW is focusing on being an Internet exchange hub that connects all telecommunication companies in one data base that serves a strong and solid connections. The purpose of this project to become a gateway of the future and becoming a communication center A carrier neutral internet exchange point operated by the internet community in Kuwait, connecting local and international content to lower costs and improve user experience.

Our Mission:

Create a regional peering hub to improve reach, assure quality, and lower bandwidth costs. Deliver enhanced customer experience to the region using a robust state of the art infrastructure to position ix.kw as a leading regional internet exchange point.

Long term goals:

    • To be the leading Internet exchange hub.

    • Low latency network

    • Reduce bandwidth cost

    • Insure accessibility for all major Internet community members
